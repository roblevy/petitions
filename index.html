DataDrivenEcon's UK Trump petition tracker.
Red is pro-Trump. Blue is anti-Trump.
When you first get here, the two petitions are started off at zero. The changes to the petition signatory count are then tracked once every two seconds or so. The total number of signatories is shown by the numbers above the chart. The chart goes between 0 and 500. This is just a quick, dirty way to use the fact that parliament.uk publishes data on its polls, a fact I find very cool indeed. But there's clearly a lot of room for improvement. If you want to make it better go to my Github and sort it out.
